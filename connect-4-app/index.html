Let's Play Connect 4!

Directions:

  1. Player 1 is red and player 2 is yellow
  2. Each player takes turns making a move
  3. Player 1 starts first.
  4. First player to get four checkers in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally wins the round. The counter below keeps track of the rounds won.
  5. Keep playing rounds for however long you'd like.
  6. You may clear the score record if you want to start your game over.
